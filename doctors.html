Our doctors

Dr. Eliud Mudiwa

Dr. Eliud Mudiwa was born and raised in Toronto. He completed her undergraduate studies in Psychology at the University of Western Ontario, and obtained his medical degree at the University of Toronto.

He continued at the University of Toronto to complete his residency in Family Medicine at the Toronto Western Hospital. He is a lecturer in the Department of Family and Community Medicine at the University of Toronto and helps to train medical students.

Dr. Mudiwa has been practicing comprehensive, patient focused care since 2003. He enjoys caring for patients throughout their lifespan, from newborns to senior citizens.

Dr. Sanjeet Chand

Dr. Sanjeet Chand was born and raised in Montreal. He completed a bachelor of science degree in Psychology at McGill university, a 2 year masters program in Nutritional Science at University of Toronto and then returned to McGill to complete his medical degree. He then completed his Family Practice residency and served as Chief Resident at Mt. Sinai Hospital through the University of Toronto.

Dr. Chand has been providing comprehensive primary care to children and adults since 1995. He mentors medical students and is also the lead physician of our Family Health Organization, Yongeline FHO.

Dr. Dorine Helen

Dr. Dorine Helen was born and raised in Toronto. She completed her undergraduate studies in Psychology at the University of Western Ontario, and then attended medical school at McMaster University.

She returned to Toronto for her residency in Family Medicine, which she completed at North York General Hospital through the University of Toronto in 2010. Dr. Helen enjoys practicing the full scope of Family Medicine, including providing care to people of all ages, from newborns to seniors. She is focused on providing comprehensive and patient-centered primary care.
